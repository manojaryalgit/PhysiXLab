Projectile Motion Simulator

How it Works

  • Enter the initial velocity, launch angle, and gravity. Optionally, you can enter an air resistance coefficient.
  • Click "Set Values" to confirm your inputs.
  • The simulation will display the trajectory of the projectile.
  • Adjust the parameters to see how they affect the range, maximum height, and time of flight.

Physics Concepts

  • Projectile Motion: The motion of an object thrown or projected into the air, subject to gravity.
  • Initial Velocity: The velocity at which the projectile is launched.
  • Launch Angle: The angle at which the projectile is launched, relative to the horizontal.
  • Gravity: The force that pulls the projectile downwards.
  • Air Resistance: A force that opposes the motion of the projectile through the air.
